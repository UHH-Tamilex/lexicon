# Peer review checklist

## Citations

* Are there any typos ― in the English or in the Tamil?
* Do the quotations, especially to external sources, correspond to the editions cited?
  * Do the quotations support the argument being made?
  * Are the English translations clear?

## Commentarial glosses

* Is the correct commentator cited, especially where multiple commentataries are on the same page?

## Meanings attested in the _Nikaṇṭu_-s

* Are any _Tivākaram_ citations marked by a star, i.e., inserted by the editor?

## Other lexica

* Do the links to other lexica point to the correct pages?
* Do any links point erroneously to a homonym?
* Are any lexica missing from the list?

## Additional comments

* Are there any other resources, such as books and articles, that could be suggested?
* Are there cognates or false cognates in other languages that are worth considering?
* Do you have further comments or suggestions?
